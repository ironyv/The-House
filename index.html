About Us

Our food is commonly described as Asian-Fusion cuisine with the typical sweet flavor, with a focus on food presentation and good service. Favorites include the nicely textured tuna poke with mini crispy rice balls, the not-too-sweet persimmon bread-pudding dessert and the fruity Pinot Noir from Ponzi Wines. Our deep-fried salmon rolls with the spicy Chinese hot mustard sauce and grilled sea bass with garlic ginger soy are renouned across the country, winning several awards.
